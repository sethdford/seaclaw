# Respond to Incident Command

Chain these steps:

1. Use `incident-response-plan` to activate IR plan and assign roles
2. Use `containment-strategy` to develop containment and stop the attack
3. Use `evidence-preservation` to collect forensic evidence while preserving chain of custody
4. Use `incident-communication` to notify stakeholders and customers
5. Use `recovery-procedures` to restore systems to normal operation

Deliverables:

- Incident response timeline and actions taken
- Containment summary and effectiveness
- Evidence collected and preserved
- Communication log with stakeholder notifications
- Recovery validation checklist

After completion, suggest follow-up commands: `investigate-breach`, `write-postmortem`.
