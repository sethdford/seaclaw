Improvement 4

Glass Choreography

Blur and saturation animate on enter/exit. Glass reveals build gradually instead of snapping. Saturation stays restrained so frost reads neutral (matches live tokens — not the old 180–200% wash).

Subtle Nav, ambient panels blur: 12px · saturate: 105% · brightness: 1.12
Standard Cards, panels, sidebars blur: 24px · saturate: 128% · brightness: 1.12
Prominent Modals, sheets, overlays blur: 32px · saturate: 148% · brightness: 1.12
