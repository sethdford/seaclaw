# Social Analytics

Interpret engagement metrics with context. Distinguish vanity metrics from outcomes (signups, revenue proxies). Account for algorithm shifts and seasonality.

Normalize by reach; cohort posts by type (video, thread, link).

## When to Use
- Growth reviews, campaign retros, or influencer reporting

## Workflow
1. Pull time-bounded data; define KPIs upfront (CTR, saves, shares).
2. Segment by content pillar and audience; note anomalies and external events.
3. Visualize trends; call out top/bottom performers with hypotheses.
4. Recommend experiments with measurable next steps.

## Examples
**Example 1:** Video spikes but link CTR flat—test CTA placement and first 3s hook.

**Example 2:** Comment sentiment shift after pricing change—qualitative sample read.
