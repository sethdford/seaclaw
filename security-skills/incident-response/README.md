# Incident Response Plugin

Incident response and breach management toolkit including IR planning, forensic analysis, incident communication, containment, and post-incident reviews.

## Skills

### 1. incident-response-plan

Develop comprehensive incident response plans with roles, escalation procedures, communication templates, and playbooks.

### 2. forensic-analysis-guide

Conduct digital forensics including evidence preservation, chain of custody, timeline reconstruction, and root cause analysis.

### 3. incident-communication

Manage internal and external communication during security incidents including notification, status updates, and disclosure.

### 4. containment-strategy

Develop containment strategies to stop active attacks, limit exposure, and prevent escalation.

### 5. evidence-preservation

Preserve digital evidence while maintaining chain of custody and legal admissibility.

### 6. root-cause-analysis-security

Conduct root cause analysis to understand how the incident occurred and systemic weaknesses.

### 7. recovery-procedures

Plan and execute recovery procedures including system restoration, credential rotation, and validation.

### 8. lessons-learned

Conduct post-incident reviews and document lessons learned to prevent recurrence.

## Commands

### respond-to-incident

Execute incident response procedures including assessment, containment, communication, and recovery.

### investigate-breach

Conduct forensic investigation of suspected breaches including evidence collection and timeline reconstruction.

### write-postmortem

Conduct post-incident review and document findings, root causes, and preventive measures.

## Quick Start

1. **Active incident**: Use `respond-to-incident` to execute IR procedures
2. **Breach investigation**: Use `investigate-breach` to conduct forensics
3. **Post-incident review**: Use `write-postmortem` to document lessons learned
