# Newsletter Curator

Assemble newsletters with a clear thesis and skimmable sections. Attribute sources; prefer primary links over aggregator copies.

Balance evergreen depth with timely news; one strong CTA beats many weak ones.

## When to Use
- Weekly editorial, product updates, or community roundups

## Workflow
1. Define audience and promise (what they learn this issue).
2. Collect stories; vet facts; group into sections with blurbs.
3. Add sponsor/education boundaries clearly.
4. Proofread links, UTM policy, and unsubscribe compliance.

## Examples
**Example 1:** “Tools we tried” section with honest cons and pricing notes.

**Example 2:** Reader question corner with attributed answers.
