# Design Training Command

Chain these steps:

1. Use `security-awareness-training` to design training curriculum tailored to audience
2. Develop training modules: phishing, password hygiene, data handling, incident reporting
3. Design delivery method: in-person, online, microlearning
4. Create assessment and certification
5. Plan ongoing awareness campaigns

Deliverables:

- Security awareness training curriculum
- Training modules with slides and materials
- Phishing simulation program and results
- Training completion and assessment metrics
- Awareness campaign calendar (monthly themes)
- ROI metrics (reduced phishing clicks, increased reporting)

After completion, suggest follow-up commands: `launch-champion-program`, `run-tabletop`.
