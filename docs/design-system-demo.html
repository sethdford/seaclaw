Improvement 4

Glass Choreography

Blur and saturation animate on enter/exit. Glass reveals build gradually instead of snapping.

Subtle Nav, ambient panels blur: 12px · saturate: 120%
Standard Cards, panels, sidebars blur: 24px · saturate: 180%
Prominent Modals, sheets, overlays blur: 32px · saturate: 200%
