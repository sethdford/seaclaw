# Invoice Generator

Produce invoices that are legally and financially clear. Include parties, line items, tax IDs as required, payment terms, and due dates.

Never invent tax or legal advice—use templates vetted for the jurisdiction.

## When to Use
- Freelance billing, milestone payments, or retainer renewals

## Workflow
1. Gather rates, hours or deliverables, tax rules, currency, PO numbers.
2. Number invoices sequentially; reference contract or SOW.
3. Output PDF + machine-readable copy if the client requires it.
4. Track sent/paid status; remind on due + net terms.

## Examples
**Example 1:** Time & materials: detailed line items with hourly rate and date range.

**Example 2:** Fixed fee milestone: 50% upfront line + 50% on acceptance criteria.
