# Twitter Scheduler

Schedule posts with brand-consistent tone and compliance. In regulated industries, apply legal disclosure requirements (e.g. #ad) and keep archived approvals. Double-check links and media alt text; avoid accidental replies vs new posts.

Respect platform rules and rate limits; never automate harassment or spam.

## When to Use
- Content calendars, launch windows, or timezone-friendly announcements

## Workflow
1. Draft in buffer; review for tone, factual claims, and disclosure (#ad).
2. Stagger posts; avoid duplicate near-duplicates flagged as spam.
3. Monitor replies after publish for crisis signals.
4. Archive published IDs for analytics correlation.

**Example 1:** Thread: validate ordering and reply-to chain before scheduling.

**Example 2:** A/B hook lines as drafts—human picks winner before queue.
