# Time Tracker

Attribute time to projects and tasks for estimation improvement. Round to practical granularity; avoid surveillance tone—focus on usefulness to the user.

Reconcile calendar blocks with logged time when inferring gaps.

## When to Use
- Client billing, capacity planning, or personal productivity reflection

## Workflow
1. Define projects/tags; start/stop timers or batch-entry at day end.
2. Review weekly: overhead vs deep work; re-tag misclassified entries.
3. Export for payroll or invoicing with audit trail.

## Examples
**Example 1:** Tag meetings by client vs internal; flag recurring overrun.

**Example 2:** Split day: 3h feature, 1h review, 1h support—note context switches.
